Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used and shared when you visit or make a purchase through the Veld Vibe website.


Veld Vibe is an independent reseller of products supplied and fulfilled by National Clothing Distributors, our trusted supplier and fulfilment partner.

Personal Information We Collect

We collect two types of information when you visit or place an order through our website:

  • Order Information: information you provide when placing an order, such as your name, email address, telephone number, billing address, shipping address and order details.
  • Device Information: information about your device, browser, IP address, cookies and how you interact with the website.

How We Collect Information

We collect information using technologies such as cookies, log files, Meta Pixel and Google Analytics.

These tools help us improve the customer experience, understand how visitors use the website and measure the effectiveness of our advertising campaigns.

How We Use Your Personal Information

We use the information collected through the website to process and fulfil orders, arrange shipping, communicate with customers and provide invoices or order confirmations where applicable.

We may also use this information to help screen orders for potential risk or fraud and to improve our products, services and website experience.

Where you have chosen to receive updates or promotional content, your information may also be used to provide marketing and advertising relating to our products and services.

Sharing Your Personal Information

To process and fulfil orders, your personal information may be shared with National Clothing Distributors, our trusted supplier and fulfilment partner, as well as PayFast, courier companies and other service providers involved in delivering your order.

We also use tools such as Google Analytics and Meta Pixel to better understand how visitors interact with our website and to improve our advertising and marketing efforts.

We may share your information where required to comply with applicable laws and regulations or to protect our rights.

Behavioural Advertising

We may use your personal information to provide you with relevant advertisements or marketing communications that may be of interest to you.

Data Retention

We will retain your order information for our records unless and until you request that this information be deleted, subject to any legal or operational requirements.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes to our business practices, operational requirements or legal obligations.

Minors

This website is not intended for individuals under the age of 16.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or would like to make a complaint, please contact us using the contact details provided on the website.
